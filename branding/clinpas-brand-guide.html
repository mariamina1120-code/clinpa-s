04 — Setting Up Your Brand Kit in Canva
1
Open Brand Hub In Canva, go to Brand Hub (left sidebar) → click "+ Add brand" or select your existing brand kit. This is where you'll store everything.
2
Upload your logos Upload clinpas-logo.svg as your primary logo, clinpas-logo-dark.svg as your dark variant, and clinpas-icon.svg as your icon/small logo. SVG keeps them crisp at any size.
3
Add brand colors Paste these hex codes one by one under Brand Colors:
#1E3A5F Navy · #0D9488 Teal Core · #14B8A6 Teal Mid · #5EEAD4 Teal Light · #CCFBF1 Teal Pale · #162742 Navy Deep · #F1F5F9 Slate Light · #64748B Slate
4
Set brand fonts Under Brand Fonts, search for Plus Jakarta Sans (it's free in Canva). Set it for Heading (ExtraBold 800), Subheading (Bold 700), and Body (Regular 400). If Plus Jakarta Sans isn't available, use DM Sans or Inter as backups.
5
Apply to templates Whenever you create a new design, click the brand kit icon in the editor toolbar to instantly apply your ClinPAS colors, fonts, and logos to any template.
